Free Guy

Director:
Shawn Levy

Description

Free Guy is a movie about a video game, the main protagonist of the movie is known as an NPC (Non-playable character) in the video game world. He's been living his whole life repeating the same routine and days over and over again but his life changed forever when he started doing things differently. The goal he set is to stop the bad guy from ruining the game and killing all of his friends in the game, and he is set to achieve this goal with the help of a real player, partnering up with her to take down the bad guys in the game and out of the game.

PG-13
